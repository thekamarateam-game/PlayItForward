Rules

Game Flow:

The game starts with players rolling the dice from the start tile, aiming to race towards the Impact Zone, which represents the achievement of playing and learning the game.

Step 1

All players place their characters at the START.

Step 2

Players take turns rolling the dice and moving their tokens the number of spaces indicated.

Step 3

When a player lands on a tile, they activate the tile effect. The cards are faced downward to keep the content hidden but remain shuffled:
